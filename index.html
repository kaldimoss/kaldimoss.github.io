Moss' Homepage

Notes, Observations, Research, Sketches
Moss previously worked under the name Sultana Zana. They are a media artist interested in ecology, networks, language and intelligence(s). Moss works with spatial sound, code, electronics, sculpture and performance.

This is a website for ongoing investigations and collaborations. Visit Whattt for shows and exhibitions, dreams and experiments for ecological explorations (2017-2019), thought caves grow fast for the-mycelium-project(2016-18) and more recently Fieldness for a ecological dapp concept + working web 2.0 prototypes (2020- 2022).
